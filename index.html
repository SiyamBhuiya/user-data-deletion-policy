User Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: 16/11/2024

Last Updated: 16/11/2024

At teset002, we respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that users have control over their personal data. This Data Deletion Policy outlines how you can request the deletion of data collected through the App.

1. How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like us to delete the data associated with your use of teset002, please follow the steps below:

  1. Contact Us: Send an email to siyam35-702@diu.edu.bd with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Provide Identification Information: To verify your identity, include:
    • The email address or Facebook user ID you used to access the App.
    • Any other information necessary for verification (e.g., approximate date of app use).
  3. Confirmation: Once we confirm your identity, we will delete your data from our servers and notify you of the completion of the process.

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Any user-provided data collected by the App.
  • Any data retrieved from Facebook with your authorization (e.g., posts, comments).

Please note: Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you will not be deleted, as it does not constitute personal information.

3. Timeframe

We aim to process all data deletion requests within 30 days of receiving your request. If there are delays due to unforeseen circumstances, we will notify you promptly.

4. Legal and Ethical Obligations

In certain cases, we may retain your data to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. In such instances, we will inform you of the reason and the duration for which the data will be retained.
